## Ad 1: Job Intake

Ad name: `ad01-job-intake`

Audience hypothesis: Operations managers, dispatch managers, crane company owners, labour allocation businesses, and lift managers who deal with rough job notes becoming rework.

Funnel stage: Cold to warm problem recognition.

Screenshot used: Public DispatchTalon screenshot plus a labelled `Pilot workflow preview` panel for Job Brief Import.

Main hook: Rough job notes should not become dispatch rework.

Intro text: Rough job notes can become reviewed job requirements before allocation.

Headline: Structure the job before dispatch

Description: Job Brief Import, requirement review, schedule, and audit trail.

CTA: Watch the portal walkthrough

Alt text: Dark DispatchTalon ad showing a Job Brief Import preview with rough job notes converted into reviewed dispatch requirements.

Visual overlay text:
- Rough job notes should not become dispatch rework.
- DispatchTalon helps turn pasted job details into reviewed requirements before allocation.
- Dispatcher reviews before the job enters allocation.

Landing page URL: `https://pressuresystems.au/liftiq/`

Risk/claim notes: Do not imply automatic job creation or final dispatch approval. Keep the review-before-allocation boundary.

Why this ad exists: Job intake is a category-entry problem. It teaches the market that the hidden cost starts before allocation.

Variant B:
- Intro text: Before dispatch, rough job details need structure.
- Headline: Turn job notes into reviewed requirements
- Description: Pilot-stage dispatch intelligence for labour and lifting teams.
- CTA: Request pilot access
