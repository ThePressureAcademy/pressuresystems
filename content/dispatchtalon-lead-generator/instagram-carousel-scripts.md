- Charcoal background `#16191d`
- Cream text `#ece6d3`

- No stock hard hats, no compliance badges, no AI graphics

## Reply playbook for DM "TALON"

1. Reply within 24h.
2. Open: "Sending the link now. One question while I do - what's the part of your dispatch loop you wish you could trust more?"
3. Send the diagnostic link with the Instagram UTM:

   ```
   ?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=dm&utm_campaign=drd-v1&utm_content=talondm
   ```
4. When they reply with their pain point, *don't* pitch. Acknowledge, then say: "When you've got the score, ping it back and I'll read it against what you just told me."
5. After they share their result: follow `follow-up-framework.md` for their band.

## Reel adaptation (optional, lower priority)

Any carousel can be adapted into a Reel by:

- Recording a 30–45 second founder-voice over each slide.
- Keeping the same hook → wedge → CTA shape.
- Captioning each frame so it's understandable on mute.

Don't run Reels until the carousel sequence has produced at least 5 diagnostic completions. Reels amplify; they don't bootstrap.

## What NOT to post on Instagram

- Memes that don't carry the wedge.
- Behind-the-scenes "founder grind" posts.
- AI-generated images of cranes.
- Pricing graphics.
- Anything that uses LIFTIQ as the active brand.
- Anything that implies DispatchTalon certifies a worker or approves a lift.
