# WhatsApp - Outreach Scripts

**Internal sales asset.** 3 scripts for warm operators only. Not for cold outbound.

Rules across all three:

- Short. Phone-screen length.
- Founder voice. First-person.
- No marketing language. No emojis (one is okay if it fits the conversation).
- No pricing.
- No claim outside the boundary list.
- Send to one person at a time. Never broadcast.

---

## Script 1 - Warm operator contact (you've met or had a real conversation)

**When to use:** You've had a conversation in the last 12 months. They know what you do at a high level. They haven't seen DispatchTalon yet.

```
Hey [First name] - Cody here.

I built a 3-minute dispatch readiness check. It shows where job intake, worker fit, role coverage, and audit usually break down before a job goes out.

Not a demo, not a pitch - just a self-score. Want me to send the link?
```

If they say **yes**:

```
Here you go: [diagnostic link with ?utm_source=whatsapp&utm_medium=dm&utm_campaign=drd-v1&utm_content=warmop]

Takes about 3 minutes. When you've got your score, send it back and I'll give you a read on it.
```

If they say **what's it for / why now**:

```
Mostly a self-check for the operators I talk to. Helps me see if DispatchTalon's a fit before I even pitch it - saves us both a meeting if it isn't.
```

If they say **send me the demo first**:

```
Easier the other way around - score the check, then I'll show you the bit of the product that maps to your result. Less generic that way.
```

## Script 2 - Existing industry mate (you know each other)

**When to use:** Real industry relationship. Trades messages occasionally. Already knows DispatchTalon exists in some form.

```
Mate - built a 3-minute dispatch readiness check.

A few of you guys have been telling me where the dispatch process gets messy. The check is the result. Score yourself, see what it pulls out, send me the result, I'll tell you what I'd actually do with it.

[diagnostic link with ?utm_source=whatsapp&utm_medium=dm&utm_campaign=drd-v1&utm_content=mate]
```

If they push back ("I don't need a tool"):

```
That's fine - if the score's high, the check confirms it and we both move on. If it's low, you know which part of dispatch is exposed. Either way it's 3 minutes.
```

If they ask **about price**:

```
Pricing's not where this is at yet - we're still picking design partners. Score first, then if it's a fit I'll walk you through what a pilot looks like.
```

## Script 3 - Operations manager / supervisor (intro via owner or peer)

**When to use:** An owner or peer has flagged the ops manager as the right person to talk to. You may not have met them directly.

```
Hi [First name] - Cody from DispatchTalon, [intro source] mentioned you run the allocation side at [Company].

Built a 3-minute self-check for operators. Covers job intake, worker / role / credential visibility, multi-role coverage, publish control, and audit. Not a demo, not a pitch - it gives a score and a read.

Worth 3 minutes? [diagnostic link with ?utm_source=whatsapp&utm_medium=dm&utm_campaign=drd-v1&utm_content=opsmgr]
```

If they reply **what's DispatchTalon**:

```
Decision-support for dispatchers. Structures the call before the job goes out - roles needed, who can cover them, credentials, override capture, audit trail. The dispatcher still makes the call. Pilot-stage product.
```

If they reply with **interest but no time**:

```
Fair - when you've got 3 minutes between jobs, the link will still be there. If you'd rather walk through it with me on a call instead of self-scoring, I can do that too.
```

If they reply **not interested**:

```
All good - appreciate the read.
```

(End the thread cleanly. Note the response in the lead sheet as "warm - not now" and revisit in 90 days.)

## Quick reference - what to do with the result

When they send back a score / band:

| Band | Your reply |
|---|---|
| 0–20 Manual Dispatch Risk | "That tracks - most of the operators we talk to score in that range. The exposure is usually role coverage and audit. Want a 20-min call to walk through one of your real recent jobs?" |
| 21–35 Partially Structured | "Useful - your structure isn't the problem, the layer behind the decision is. Want to send a sample job through DispatchTalon with me?" |
| 36–48 Dispatch-Ready but Exposed | "You're more structured than most. Worth a focused pilot scenario on one allocation window - I'll show you exactly what changes." |
| 49–60 Pilot Candidate | "That's a design-partner-grade score. Want me to send the design partner pack and we set up a scoping call?" |

Then follow `follow-up-framework.md` for the full cadence.

## What NOT to send on WhatsApp

- Long paragraphs.
- Pricing.
- Multi-link messages.
- Voice notes (unless they send one first).
- Images or PDFs without context.
- Anything that uses LIFTIQ as the active brand.
- Any auto-send / mass-broadcast. The channel only works when the relationship is real.
