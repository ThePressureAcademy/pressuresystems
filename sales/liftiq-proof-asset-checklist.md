# DispatchTalon Proof Asset Checklist

![DispatchTalon by Pressure Systems](../assets/logos/dispatchtalon/dispatchtalon-primary-lockup.png)

Use proof assets to answer a specific belief gap. Do not show everything by default.

| Asset | When to use | What to say | What not to say |
|---|---|---|---|
| Public DispatchTalon page | Positioning and boundaries | DispatchTalon is pilot-stage dispatch intelligence. | Do not use it as detailed product proof. |
| Portal walkthrough recording | Short calls or unstable portal | This is the clean workflow. | Do not call it a VSL publicly. |
| Screenshots | Backup proof | These are controlled proof points. | Do not imply live customer data. |
| Demo tenant | Live demo with clean data | This shows current portal capability safely. | Do not show real tenant data. |
| Worker import screenshot | Labour-only prospects | Worker lists can be imported instead of re-keyed. | Do not imply HR or payroll system. |
| Our Business screenshot | Company setup relevance | The portal can be configured around what the company uses. | Do not imply public self-serve signup. |
| Labour-only mode screenshot | Labour suppliers | Plant clutter can be hidden by default. | Do not make DispatchTalon generic HR software. |
| Plant + labour mode screenshot | Crane/plant operators | Plant and people context can sit in the same decision flow. | Do not claim full fleet management. |
| Asset register screenshot | Plant-number objection | Multiple machines can sit under the same equipment class. | Do not imply maintenance or telematics. |
| Job form filtered requirements screenshot | Intake complexity | The job form can show relevant options. | Do not imply the system decides what is required. |
| One-off requirement screenshot | Edge cases | One-off notes stay job-scoped for review. | Do not imply catalogue pollution or automation. |
| Job Brief Import screenshot | Rough-note pain | Rough notes become structured suggestions for review. | Do not imply autonomous job creation. |
| SmartRank screenshot | Recommendation proof | Ranking is explainable and reviewable. | Do not imply the system makes the decision. |
| CredentialGate/FatigueGuard screenshot | Readiness checks | Warnings and blocks surface before allocation. | Do not say safe to dispatch. |
| Crane/counterweight planning screenshot | Plant + labour prospects | Counterweight and transport review flags are visible. | Do not imply permit approval or legal road access. |
| Audit screenshot | Proof trail | The decision trail is recorded. | Do not imply regulatory guarantee. |
| Metrics screenshot | Pilot evidence | Pilot activity can be reviewed. | Do not imply customer ROI without evidence. |

## Minimum Asset Set Before First Serious Demo

- Public DispatchTalon page
- 3-minute portal walkthrough or screenshots
- Our Business screenshot
- Labour-only mode screenshot
- Plant + labour mode screenshot
- Asset register screenshot
- Job Brief Import screenshot
- SmartRank screenshot
- Audit screenshot
- Metrics screenshot

## Privacy Rules

- No passwords.
- No JWTs.
- No Fly secrets.
- No tenant admin emails unless specifically approved for that recipient.
- No real employee data.
- No real customer job notes.
- Use sample/demo data only.
