# Design Partner Follow-up Templates

**Use:** Send within hours of the relevant trigger, not days. Each template assumes the matching outbound or call has already happened. Subjects are written for email; for LinkedIn drop the subject and use the body.

**Tone:** Direct, operator-aware, no hype. Same vocabulary register as the outreach messages.

---

## Hygiene rules

- One follow-up channel at a time. Email then LinkedIn then call — not all three in 24 hours.
- Three nudges maximum on cold outreach. After that, drop into the re-engagement file with a six-month timer.
- Never attach the agreement on a first message. Scope first.
- Never quote a price without quoting the scope alongside it.
- Never use words from the banned list (revolutionary · AI replaces dispatch · compliant · safe to dispatch · Xero integrated · payroll-ready · guaranteed · automatically prevents incidents).

---

*Templates are starting points, not scripts. Match the message to the partner. The quote is the thing that makes the message real — use one from the actual conversation whenever possible.*
