# DispatchTalon Demo Talk Track

## Opening

DispatchTalon is not trying to replace dispatch judgement. It structures the decision before dispatch: job notes, workers, credentials, fatigue, schedule pressure, plant context where needed, review flags, and the audit trail.

The question is not whether the portal has every future feature. The question is whether it gives dispatch a clearer decision path than memory, spreadsheets, and rushed phone calls.

## 3-Minute Short Demo

1. Public page
   - Say: "This is the category and boundary."
   - Do not say: "This is a finished enterprise platform."
2. Job Brief Import
   - Say: "Rough notes become structured suggestions for review."
   - Do not say: "The system creates jobs autonomously."
3. SmartRank
   - Say: "The recommendation is explainable."
   - Do not say: "The system makes the call."
4. Audit
   - Say: "The reasoning is kept in the trail."
   - Do not say: "This proves legal or compliance coverage."
5. CTA
   - Say: "If this matches your pressure, the next step is a controlled test or managed pilot."

## Labour-Only Demo

Use when the prospect mainly supplies people.

Flow:
1. Dashboard
2. Our Business set to Labour only
3. Worker import
4. Credentials and VOCs
5. Job Brief Import
6. Scheduled job
7. SmartRank
8. CredentialGate and FatigueGuard
9. Allocation with override reason
10. Audit
11. Metrics

Talk track:

> This path stays focused on people. Workers, roles, tickets, VOCs, fatigue, availability, schedule, preferences, recommendations, and audit. If you do not own plant, the portal should not force plant setup into your daily workflow.

## Plant + Labour Demo

Use when the prospect allocates people and plant.

Flow:
1. Dashboard
2. Our Business set to Plant + labour
3. Company requirement catalogue
4. Asset register
5. Multiple assets under one class
6. Job Brief Import
7. Crane / counterweight / transport review
8. Schedule
9. SmartRank
10. Allocation
11. Audit
12. Metrics

Talk track:

> This is where DispatchTalon separates equipment class from actual plant. A job may require a 20T mobile crane, while the company has MC20-001, MC20-002, MC20-003, and MC20-004. DispatchTalon can carry that context without pretending to be full fleet management.

## Crane / Counterweight / Transport Boundary

Say:

> DispatchTalon can surface crane model, travel state, counterweight gap, support transport, and road-access review flags. It does not approve permits, confirm legal road access, or engineer the lift.

## Closing

For a light test, keep the scope small. For a real setup with imports, company configuration, support, weekly review, and outcome tracking, frame it as a managed pilot.
