# DispatchTalon Discovery Question Bank

![DispatchTalon by Pressure Systems](../assets/logos/dispatchtalon/dispatchtalon-primary-lockup.png)

## Accusation Audit

- You might be thinking this is another tool that creates admin.
- You might be thinking your dispatchers already know this stuff.
- You might be thinking software should not be making crane or labour allocation decisions. I agree.
- You might be thinking this sounds like fleet management. It is not full fleet management.
- You might be thinking this is too early-stage. That may be true depending on what you need.

## Labour-Only Questions

- Do you mainly allocate people, plant, or both?
- How are workers currently matched to jobs?
- How do you track credentials, VOCs, rail tickets, WAH, or site requirements?
- How do you know who is preferred for certain jobs?
- How do you handle availability and fatigue?
- How far ahead are people allocated?
- What causes the most changes after the first allocation?
- Where is the reasoning behind the allocation recorded?
- If someone else had to allocate tomorrow, what would they not know?
- What job type creates the most rework?

## Plant + Labour Questions

- Do you allocate by equipment class, specific plant number, or both?
- If you have several cranes of the same size, how do you track which one is assigned?
- How do you capture crane setup, counterweight, support transport, or site-access notes?
- Where do road-access review flags get recorded?
- How do you avoid double-booking workers or plant?
- What happens when the equipment requirement changes late?
- How do you track plant availability if not using a full fleet system?
- Who confirms whether support transport is needed?
- Where does plant number history live today?

## General Commercial Questions

- What system are you using now?
- Where does it work?
- Where does it fall over?
- Who feels the pain first?
- Who would need to approve a pilot?
- What would make a 14-day test useful?
- What would make a 90-day pilot worth paying for?
- What is the cost of doing nothing for another quarter?
- What support would you expect during setup?
- What data would need to be clean before a pilot?

## Consequence Questions

- What happens when a credential issue is found late?
- What happens when a tired worker looks like the easiest option?
- What happens when a plant assumption is wrong?
- What happens when the original allocation reasoning is challenged later?
- How often does the first plan change before the job starts?

## Future-State Questions

- If the decision trail was visible, who would care most?
- If job notes became structured faster, what would that change?
- If the portal only solved one issue, which one would be worth paying for?
- What would make dispatch say this is easier than the current way?

## Decision-Process Questions

- Who else needs to see this?
- Who owns the risk if the current workflow fails?
- Who owns the budget for a pilot?
- What would block a 90-day test?
- If we agreed the scope, when could a pilot realistically start?
