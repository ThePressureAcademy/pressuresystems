The problem

Most dispatch risk starts before the job reaches site.

Job notes, worker suitability, credentials, fatigue, schedules, plant numbers, travel, and office handoff often sit across messages, spreadsheets, OneNote, memory, and phone calls.

Intake

Rough notes become unclear job requirements.

DispatchTalon turns notes into reviewed roles, credentials, site conditions, travel flags, plant context, and additional requirements.

Suitability

Worker fit lives in someone's head.

Credentials, VOCs, fatigue, availability, schedule, and task preferences become visible before allocation.

Handoff

Allocation reasoning is rarely captured.

SmartRank, override reasons, publish previews, audit events, and exports keep the decision trail available after the call.

Position: DispatchTalon does not replace dispatch conversations. It turns them into a structured workflow.
