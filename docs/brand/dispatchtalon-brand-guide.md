## Spacing Rules

Use clearspace equal to at least one decision-node diameter around the icon.

For the horizontal lockup, keep clearspace equal to the height of the lowercase parent-brand line above, below, and to both sides.

Do not place the mark inside tight boxes, decorative badges, heavy outlines, or busy photography without a dark overlay.

## Dark Background Use

Preferred use is on dark graphite or near-black backgrounds.

Use:

- full-colour lockup on dark graphite
- full-colour icon on dark graphite
- monochrome white version when colour reproduction is limited

The muted-rust decision node should stay visible in full-colour applications.

## Light Background Use

Use the monochrome dark version on light backgrounds when the full-colour mark loses contrast.

If using the full-colour icon on light backgrounds, place it inside the approved dark app-icon container or a controlled dark graphite panel.

## Minimum Size

Recommended minimum sizes:

- Primary horizontal lockup: 180 px wide
- Website header lockup: 150 px wide
- Icon-only mark: 32 px wide
- Favicon/app icon: use the simplified app-icon or favicon files below 32 px
- LinkedIn ad corner mark: 120 px wide

At very small sizes, use:

- `dispatchtalon-app-icon-1024.png`
- `dispatchtalon-favicon-512.png`
- `dispatchtalon-app-icon.svg`
- `dispatchtalon-favicon.svg`

## Do Not

- Do not stretch, skew, rotate, or distort the mark.
- Do not recolour outside the approved Pressure Systems palette.
- Do not replace the muted-rust decision node with blue, green, neon, or gradient colours.
- Do not add bevels, shadows, chrome effects, textures, or 3D mockup styling.
- Do not place the mark inside a gaming-style shield.
- Do not add a bird mascot, animal foot, crane hook, AI brain, or random monogram.
- Do not redraw the typography with a different product spelling.
- Do not use the old LIFTIQ mark for current DispatchTalon public, portal, sales, or campaign surfaces.

## Parent Brand Line

Use exact wording:

```text
DispatchTalon
by Pressure Systems
```

Do not use:

- Dispatch Talon
- Dispatchtalon
- Dispatch-Talon
- LIFTIQ
- LiftIQ

## Notes

The current public route and Fly app may still contain `liftiq` as technical identifiers until a separate route/domain migration is approved. Those identifiers are not brand usage.
