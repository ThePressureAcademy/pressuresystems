# DispatchTalon Working Name Decision

Date: 2026-05-14

## Verdict

DispatchTalon is selected as the preferred working replacement product name for the dispatch intelligence platform previously developed as LIFTIQ.

This is a working brand migration decision only. It does not claim legal clearance, trade mark registration, or trade mark protection.

## Selected Name

Preferred product name:

> DispatchTalon

Preferred full reference:

> DispatchTalon by Pressure Systems

Category line:

> Dispatch intelligence for labour and lifting operations.

Core positioning:

> DispatchTalon helps labour, crane, rigging, plant, transport, and lifting teams structure allocation decisions before dispatch.

Trust line:

> DispatchTalon does not replace dispatch judgement. It structures the decision before dispatch, shows the reasoning, and keeps the audit trail.

## Why This Name

DispatchTalon was chosen as the strongest working candidate because it:

- Describes the operating category quickly.
- Avoids the Lift/IQ construction.
- Works for labour-only and plant + labour workflows.
- Carries an industrial feel without implying compliance, permit approval, legal road access, lift engineering, payroll, or autonomous dispatch.
- Fits under the Pressure Systems parent brand.

## Boundaries

Do not say:

- DispatchTalon is legally clear.
- DispatchTalon is trade mark protected.
- LIFTIQ is infringing.
- The rename is complete at route/domain/environment level.

Approved transition language:

- DispatchTalon is the new working product name.
- DispatchTalon was previously developed as LIFTIQ.
- This is a controlled brand transition.
- Attorney review remains recommended.

## Next Legal Step

Send the top-10 candidate pack and this selected working-name decision to a trade mark attorney for clearance advice.
