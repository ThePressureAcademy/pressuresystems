## Use Rules

Use these documents to prepare advisor conversations only after founder review.

Do not treat them as:

- public collateral
- investor-ready material
- grant material
- legal, compliance, tax, or funding advice
- proof of endorsement
- proof of market uniqueness

## Claim Boundaries

Approved framing:

- pilot-stage
- decision support
- human confirmed
- dispatcher confirms
- suitability before availability
- reviewable reasoning
- operational record
- assisted setup
- current research suggests
- appears underserved
- advisor review requested

Do not claim:

- autonomous dispatch
- compliance approval
- certified site log
- legal attendance record
- safety approval
- payroll approval
- AI automatic import
- AI replaces the dispatcher
- CICA endorsement
- Australian Made endorsement
- sovereign hosting
- grant eligibility
- R&D eligibility
- no competitor exists
- guaranteed ROI

## Privacy Boundaries

Do not include:

- passwords
- tokens
- API keys
- stored upload keys
- private pilot data
- real uploaded source documents
- real worker credentials
- advisor private information
- internal admin material

Advisor demos should use clean demo tenants and fake sample data only.

## Founder Review Checklist

Before sending any brief:

1. Confirm the advisor audience and lens.
2. Remove detail that is not useful for that advisor.
3. Confirm no private data is present.
4. Confirm no overclaiming language is present.
5. Confirm competitor detail is abstracted unless the audience needs the technical appendix.
6. Confirm the brief is still framed as advisor challenge, not public promotion.
