## Use Rules

These briefs are shorter, cleaner versions of internal advisor material. They are designed for selected advisor review after a warm message or before a call.

They are not automatically approved for external use.

Before sending:

1. Cody must read and approve the exact version.
2. Remove or adjust anything that does not fit the specific advisor.
3. Confirm no private pilot data, real credentials, passwords, advisor private information, or internal admin material is present.
4. Confirm the brief asks for judgement and challenge, not endorsement.
5. Confirm no claim implies compliance approval, safety certification, autonomous dispatch, payroll approval, grant eligibility, R&D eligibility, sovereign hosting, Australian Made endorsement, CICA endorsement, market exclusivity, or guaranteed ROI.

## Current Boundary

This folder contains founder-review-ready send drafts only.

Public collateral, investor material, grant material, endorsement material, and broad industry distribution require separate approval.
