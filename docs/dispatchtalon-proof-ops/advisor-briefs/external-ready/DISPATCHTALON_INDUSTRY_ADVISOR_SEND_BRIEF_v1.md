## What DispatchTalon Is

DispatchTalon is an Australian-built dispatch intelligence layer for crane, rigging, plant, labour, transport, construction, and field-operations businesses.

It helps review the decision before dispatch by bringing together:

- availability
- suitability
- worker credentials
- asset context
- site or job requirements
- daily site records
- reviewable reasoning

The core frame is:

> Suitability before availability.

The core line is:

> The reasoning layer before the job goes out.

The dispatcher still confirms the decision.

## What DispatchTalon Is Not

DispatchTalon is not:

- autonomous dispatch
- compliance approval
- safety certification
- legal attendance certification
- payroll or timesheet approval
- lift engineering approval
- a full ERP replacement
- an automatic document-import system

It is decision support. The responsible operator remains accountable for the final call.

The aim is not to replace an experienced dispatcher. The aim is to make the reasoning clearer, more reviewable, and less dependent on memory or scattered records.

## Why This May Matter To The Industry

In crane, rigging, plant, labour, and transport work, availability is rarely the whole answer.

A worker or asset may be available but still need review because of:

- credential or induction status
- role fit
- asset suitability
- site or client requirement
- workload or timing pressure
- changed site conditions
- operational notes that only one person remembers

Many businesses manage this through phone calls, messages, spreadsheets, whiteboards, folders, and one experienced allocator holding the logic in their head.

DispatchTalon is being shaped around that gap: making the decision visible before the job goes out and keeping a practical record after the work happens.
